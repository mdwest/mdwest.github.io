Is Your Workplace Loud?

You may have a right to monetary compensation

Most have gone to a music show, or turned their headphones up for a favorite song. And maybe even damaged your hearing at one point. But, if you work in a loud environment or wear a telephone headset at work, you are at risk of long term exposure to harmful noise that could seriously affect you ability to hear.



Hearing loss is progressive, and does not always present with ringing in your ears other other obvious signs. If think you may have hearing loss, it is important to measure your hearing to prevent further harm. Fortunately, many hearing aid centers offer free hearing tests. Contact me below and I can help you find a free hearing test near you. If you are found to have hearing loss, Workers Compensation can provide you with hearing aids to restore hearing loss and a pension to compensate you for the loss of hearing.
