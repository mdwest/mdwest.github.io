A Working Class Attorney

Meet Mitch

  • Mitchell D. West

  • Attorney at Law

I know what a hard day’s work feels like. I have worked in lawn care, unloaded semi-trucks, and stocked retail shelves. These jobs gave me the work ethic necessary to earn full-tuition scholarships and graduate from law school. Now I bring that same work ethic to every client and every case I take.

I also remember why my family pushed me to pursue education. They wanted me to have an easier life. One with the pride of a job well done, but without the physical toll so many jobs take. That physical toll is why I focus my practice on workers compensation. But, as I have developed my practice, I have found more ways to serve. I take pride in fighting for consumers rights and standing up to shady businesses. I enjoy helping families plan for the future. In short, I enjoy helping people.

If you or someone you know could use help, feel free to call me. If you don’t reach me immediately, please fill out the contact form to schedule a time to chat.
