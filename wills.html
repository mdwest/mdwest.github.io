Planning Ahead

Its easy to put off planning for the the future. But we are all human, and know that one day our time in this world will end. Planning for that end can bring peace of mind and ensure the process is as simple and stress free for your loved ones.

I offer simple wills starting at $300. This document ensures your estate passes smoothly to those you love. If you have more complex needs, I can draft more advanced trusts to manage your assets but for most working class families, a simple will is enough to ensure your passing is as stress free for your loved ones as possible.
